Presentation of my doctoral project in front of the PhD faculty, summarizing the main findings of my thesis. I discussed how somesthetic signals contribute to the conscious processing of facial expressions, using evidence from binocular rivalry paradigms to show how mimicry and sensorimotor feedback modulate visual awareness of others' emotional expressions.
